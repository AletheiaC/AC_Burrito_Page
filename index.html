First things first: get yourself a good ol'tortilla. The most common types are corn and flour.

And don't you ever forget the main filling! Pork, egg, fish, beef, and more...it's all good!

The toppings are everything. Get some lettuce, a little pico de gallo, maybe a bag of cheese, tomato... the options are endless!

Finally of course, who could forget to have a delectable taco sauce, maybe its just red or green chile, or maybe its a sweet or spicy salsa.
